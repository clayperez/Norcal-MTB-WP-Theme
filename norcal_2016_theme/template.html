Race call ups

Before each race, new call ups will be provided below.  For the first race, the call ups are based on a rider's overall individual points at the end of the last season.  For the following races, call ups are based on a rider's overall individual results.  For all races after race #2, the a rider's worst result will be dropped.    [ NorCal Call Up Procedures here ].
