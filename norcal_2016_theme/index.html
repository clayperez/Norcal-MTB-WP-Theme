Register

And the season begins

Rider registration is now officially open in the Pit Zone.  Work with your coaches to make sure your team is registered, and then register yourself as a rider.  Scholarships are available, if you are interested reach out to Vanessa.

Volunteer

We promise it's fun

Volunteers are a crucial part to making the races happen.  Each race requires at least 50 volunteers.  Whether it's riding laps with the participants, or yelling race numbers in the timing tent, volunteers are everywhere.  Lending a helping hand by volunteering is a great way to experience alongside the racers.  We encourage you to sign up ahead of time, or visit the volunteering booth at the venue.
